\begin{frame}{Future Work}
    Beyond the "Dense $Y$" extension, the authors identify several promising directions for future research:
    
    \vspace{0.3cm}
    \begin{enumerate}
        \item \textbf{Irregular Intervals:} Adapting the spectral transformations for time series data that is not sampled uniformly.
        \item \textbf{General Noise:} Extending the theoretical consistency proofs to handle heteroskedastic (non-constant variance) noise distributions.
        \item \textbf{Confidence Intervals:} Developing provably valid confidence intervals rather than relying solely on heuristic methods.
    \end{enumerate}
\end{frame}
